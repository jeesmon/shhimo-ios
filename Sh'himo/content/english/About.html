Sh'himo

The MGOCSM of North America, the youth ministry of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church, makes the beautiful prayers of the West Syriac Tradition available to our faithful in North America and worldwide. It is our hope, these prayers which are made available, will instill the discipline of prayer in the lives of all our faithful, to make them strong, witnessing Orthodox Christians. The liturgical prayers for all liturgical offices from Monday to Saturday are included in this app.  The prayers of Sunday are not included, as it is the practice for Orthodox Christians to pray the liturgical hours of Sunday with the worshipping community.

May the power of the sacred prayers of the Holy Church, help us in our journey to the Kingdom of God.

The Sh'himo Prayers found in this app, are selected prayers from the Sh'himo Namaskaram, the canonical offices of prayer, as prayed in the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church, one of the ancient Oriental Orthodox Churches, belonging to the West Syriac Liturgical Tradition.
